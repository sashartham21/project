Jobty

A dynamic mobile application that combines the networking capabilities of LinkedIn with the simplicity of Tinder, designed to match job seekers with employers based on professional profiles and preferences.

Learn more

Jobty is an innovative mobile application developed using Dart, blending the best features of LinkedIn and Tinder to create a unique, streamlined job-searching experience. The app is designed to help job seekers and employers connect quickly by swiping through job opportunities and candidate profiles, providing a fun, fast, and engaging way to match the right candidates with the right positions.

Job seekers can create professional profiles, including their skills, experience, and preferences for job roles. Employers can also set up their profiles with specific job openings, along with details on company culture, location, and job requirements. The app's intelligent algorithm matches job seekers and employers based on shared interests, skills, and location, offering a seamless matching process.

In addition to the core functionality, Jobty includes built-in chat features, allowing real-time communication between candidates and employers. The app's design, created in Figma, is optimized for a clean, user-friendly interface, with intuitive navigation and easy-to-understand functionality. The mobile-first approach ensures that users have a smooth experience on all devices.

Jobty also prioritizes inclusivity by implementing accessibility features, making sure that users with diverse abilities can easily navigate and interact with the app. The project showcases skills in mobile development with Dart, UI/UX design, and an understanding of how to merge the functionalities of two widely used platforms to create a brand new solution for the job market.

This app demonstrates a deep understanding of the recruitment process and user-centered design, aiming to simplify and enhance the job search experience. With its combination of professional networking and easy-to-use features, Jobty offers an innovative solution to the way job seekers and employers connect.

SkillLink

An online platform connecting individuals with unique skills to those seeking help or learning, featuring a user-friendly interface and a rating system.

Learn more

SkillLink is a comprehensive web application aimed at bridging the gap between individuals with unique skills and those who need assistance or want to learn new things. Users can create personalized profiles, detailing their expertise in fields such as IT, design, music, and more. The platform includes robust search functionality with filtering options to ensure users can easily find relevant experts or opportunities.

One of the core features of SkillLink is its built-in rating and review system, which encourages quality service and helps users make informed decisions. Additionally, the platform incorporates a secure, integrated chat system for seamless communication between users.

SkillLink is developed with a strong focus on accessibility and usability, meeting WCAG Level AA standards to ensure inclusivity for all users. This project demonstrates expertise in front-end development using HTML, CSS, and responsive design principles, alongside a thoughtful approach to user experience and interface design. The platform also showcases an understanding of community-driven systems and highlights the importance of connecting people in a professional yet approachable environment.
